How the calc works
  1. Adjust each parent's gross income (subtract self-employment tax, other-children credit, court-ordered support paid).
  2. Sum to Combined Adjusted Gross Income (cAGI).
  3. Look up Basic Child Support Obligation (BCSO) for cAGI & child count.
  4. Add monthly add-ons (health-insurance premium for child, work-related childcare, recurring uninsured medical) to get Adjusted Support Obligation.
  5. Each parent pays their pro-rata share of the obligation by income.
  6. Parenting-time adjustment: if Alternate Residential Parent (ARP) has ≥92 days/yr, apply variable-multiplier credit (BCSO × 1.75 × ARP-day-share).
  7. Credit each parent for add-ons they directly pay; net the result; ARP pays PRP the balance.
